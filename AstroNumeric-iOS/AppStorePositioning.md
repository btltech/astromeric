# AstroNumeric App Store Positioning

## One-Line Position

Private astrology and numerology for daily timing, chart insight, and AI-guided reflection.

## App Subtitle

Private astrology and numerology

## Short Description

AstroNumeric blends birth chart astrology, numerology, practical timing, widgets, journaling, and optional AI guidance in a local-first iOS experience designed for private self-reflection.

## Full Description Draft

AstroNumeric helps you understand your chart, your numbers, and the timing of your day without turning your birth data into a social feed.

Create a private birth profile to unlock natal placements, life path insights, daily guidance, moon context, compatibility, timing windows, journaling, habits, and an AI guide that can explain patterns in plain language.

Core features:

- Local-first profile storage for birth details and preferences.
- Birth chart placements powered by bundled ephemeris data where available.
- Numerology profiles with life path and core number insights.
- Daily, weekly, and monthly guidance.
- Practical timing tools for decisions, calls, launches, recovery, and reflection.
- Moon phase, tarot, oracle, affirmation, and birthstone tools.
- Compatibility and relationship history.
- Journaling with voice transcription support.
- Lock Screen and Home Screen widgets for daily context.
- Optional AI guidance for deeper explanations.
- Privacy mode for redacted profile details and safer sharing.

AstroNumeric is built for people who want more depth than a generic horoscope, more privacy than a social astrology app, and more practical direction than vague predictions.

## Differentiation Notes

- Lead with privacy and local-first storage.
- Keep the tone practical: guidance, timing, reflection, planning.
- Avoid claiming prediction, certainty, medical benefit, or financial decision authority.
- Position AI as optional explanation and reflection, not as a replacement for professional advice.
